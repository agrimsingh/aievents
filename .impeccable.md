## Design Context

### Users

Singapore’s AI builders, researchers, and curious folks scanning for what’s on — often on a phone between meetings or late evening. Mix of founders, students, and community organizers listing or attending events.

### Brand Personality

Welcoming, polished, grassroots — a tended community board, not a corporate conference site or generic aggregator.

### Aesthetic Direction

Dark **harbor-night** theme (OKLCH): deep blue-slate surfaces (neutrals tinted ~245–250°), **jade** primary accent for actions and dates, **coral** for secondary chips — no brown/ochre bases. Bricolage Grotesque + Figtree unchanged. Editorial layout: featured “next up”, then list. Very subtle radial depth behind the hero (atmosphere, not gradient text). Sticky filter strip for scan-while-scrolling. Motion: staggered entrance; hover = transform/opacity only; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

### Design Principles

1. **Cool base, warm accents** — slate ink background; jade + coral as rare highlights (no neon cyan–purple stacks, no gradient text).
2. **Hierarchy through space** — varied spacing; flat list rows, no card-in-card clutter.
3. **Trust the source** — Luma link-out stays obvious.
4. **Readable at night** — line-height and contrast tuned for OLED/dim use.
